Perth's Solar Advantage

With an average of 3,200 hours of sunshine per year, Perth is one of the best cities in the world for solar power. But even in our sunny paradise, seasonal variations affect your system's output. Here's how to optimise performance year-round.

Did you know? Perth receives approximately 50% more solar radiation than cities like Sydney or Melbourne, making it ideal for solar power generation.

Top Tips for Year-Round Optimisation

1. Keep Panels Clean

Perth's dry, dusty conditions mean regular cleaning is essential. Aim for 2-4 cleans per year, or after dust storms.

2. Monitor Your System

Use your inverter app to track daily production. Sudden drops may indicate issues needing attention.

3. Shift Your Usage

Run dishwashers, washing machines, and pool pumps during 9am-3pm when generation peaks.

4. Consider Battery Storage

Store excess summer generation for evening use, maximising self-consumption rates.

Dealing with Perth's Unique Challenges

Sea Breeze (The Fremantle Doctor)

Perth's famous afternoon sea breeze actually helps your panels! The cooling effect in summer afternoons improves efficiency. However, salt spray near the coast means more frequent cleaning may be needed.

Dust and Bushfire Season

Smoke and dust can temporarily reduce output. After significant dust events or nearby bushfires, schedule a panel clean to restore optimal performance.

Occasional Hail

Quality solar panels are tested to withstand hail up to 25mm in diameter. All panels installed by Clear Sky Solar exceed these standards. If you experience severe hail, we recommend a visual inspection.

When to Call for Professional Help

Contact your solar provider if you notice:

  • Output drops by more than 20% compared to the same period last year
  • Physical damage to panels, wiring, or the inverter
  • Error messages on your inverter display
  • Hot spots or discolouration on panels
